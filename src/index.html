Pyronica is a fire organ, that is, a pipe organ that voices with propane instead of air (or steam for a calliope). It has three 12-note octaves and plays from any MIDI source, usually a keyboard. As far as we know, this is the first fire organ that can be played as a musical instrument. We would love to collaborate with you on making Pyronica bigger and better, or help you leverage what we've learned to make yours better. Let us know what you think!
